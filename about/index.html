About Me

Who are you?

I am a young mind residing in Montréal, Québec, Canada. I recently graduated from the John Abbott College Computer Science Career Program, which gives me all the technical skills and know-how to meet or exceed industry standards. I work hard and try to learn something new every day. I love the Internet, computers, problem-solving, coding, designing, testing, and building things. I am dedicated to the field of computer science and passionate about web development.

What do you do?

I create things with my imagination. I want to build web sites and applications that simplify our daily lives and improve quality of life for everyone. I focus on the user experience, ease-of-use, and accessibility for all. Lately I have been working on my personal projects, such as this site and my OpenShift site. I read about existing and emerging technologies.

What do you enjoy?

I have interests in many things. Too many things to put here. Here is a sample size of things I like:
  • A comfy chair and a choice keyboard that I can spend hours coding with.
  • Listening to and producing music.
  • The process of engineering, designing, and developing software.
